Contract

Define failure modes up front

Predictable failures are easier to recover from.
In the docs, explicitly list:
- auth failures (401)
- rate limits (429)
- validation errors (400)
- upstream outages/timeouts

Then provide:
- retry policy
- backoff
- what to log
- what never to log
